What is billing software?
Billing software refers to computer programs that handle the tracking of billable products and services delivered to a customer or set of customers. It helps in creating invoices and financial statements, managing accounts receivable, and ensuring that payments are collected in time. Such software is crucial for streamlining the billing process, improving efficiency, and reducing errors in financial operations.
